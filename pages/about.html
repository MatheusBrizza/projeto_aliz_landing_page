Our Story

How We Started

Aliz Costura Criativa began in 1998 when our founder, Maria Visconti, decided to follow her passion for textiles and craftsmanship. What started as a small workshop in her garden shed has grown into a thriving atelier with a team of skilled artisans.

We believe in creating products that are not only beautiful but also sustainable and made to last. Every item that leaves our workshop has been crafted with attention to detail and a commitment to quality.

Our Process

1

Design

Every product begins with a thoughtful design process, where we consider functionality, aesthetics, and sustainability.

2

Material Selection

We source only the finest materials, with a preference for organic, locally-sourced inputs that minimize environmental impact.

3

Craftsmanship

Our skilled artisans bring each design to life using traditional techniques combined with modern innovation.
